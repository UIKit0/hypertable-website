Baidu, China’s Leading Search Engine, Officially Sponsors Hypertable  

Baidu Selects Hypertable As Its Database Storage Technology 
 

San Mateo, CA - January 22, 2009 – Baidu.com, Inc. (Nasdaq: BIDU), the leading Chinese language Internet search provider, today announced that it has become an official sponsor of Hypertable, an open source, high performance distributed data storage system, specifically designed to support web-scale applications.  As a sponsor of Hypertable, Baidu has committed an industrious team of engineers, numerous servers, and support resources to improve the quality and development of the open source technology.  

"Baidu’s sponsorship is key to our long term viability, which will benefit the greater open source and developer community," said Doug Judd, Lead Architect for Hypertable. "We are grateful for the quality contributions made by the Baidu engineering team, and we look forward to continuing the collaboration” 

Hypertable, modeled after Google’s Bigtable, is designed to manage the storage and processing of information on a large cluster of commodity servers, providing resilience to machine and component failures.  Hypertable is also sponsored by Zvents, a local event-based search engine. 

About Hypertable

Established in 2007, Hypertable is an open source, high performance, scalable database designed to run on a large cluster of commodity servers.  Hypertable seeks to set the open source standard for highly available, petabyte scale, database systems.  For more information, please see http://www.hypertable.org. 

About Baidu

Baidu.com, Inc. is the leading Chinese language Internet search provider. As a technology-based media company, Baidu aims to provide the best way for people to find information.
